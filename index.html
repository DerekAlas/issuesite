In 2019, filmmaker Martin Scorsese made the controversial claim that Marvel movies were not art, they were amusement parks -- vessels made for the sole purpose of entertainment. As one can imagine, this was a very polarizing take.

While it might not be fair to dismiss these types of films completely, there is some unfortunate truth behind his opinion.

As we move further into an age where franchises, sequels, and remakes increase, the value and vision of the artist decreases. This isn’t about stopping comic book films, it’s about giving artists a chance to produce their original work.
